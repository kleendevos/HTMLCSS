Design guidelines

The houses we build, will

  1. be appropriate to a rural landscape
  2. have rooflines that complement the building site’s topography
  3. be horizontal in appearance rather than vertical
  4. use landscaping to soften the effect of the house
  5. use high quality materials
  6. use natural materials wherever possible
  7. use natural or recessive colours
